Overview of this first edition 2016-2017

The idea of ​​an annual inter-university competition in mechanical engineering (aerospace application) received a very favorable reception among several professors of Algerian universities. This scientific activity aims to offer students an opportunity to put into practice their theoretical knowledge acquired.

This edition consists of the design of a high power amateur rocket with a recovery system. It will cover four areas:

1) Design and simulation

2) Realization

3) Validation test of design concept

4) Safety aspect

The students are asked to form teams or a specification is provided to them. A drive is available including documentation needed to complete the required tasks. Examination of the security test is provided as students will be examined and a pre-established grade will be required as a passing grade or the team will be disqualified.

The competition will be organized over a day. Students will present their work to a committee composed of several jurors after the passing of the security examination. A clear and detailed scoring system will be published on the site.

Nota: No Launch is authorized or will be performed in this 2017 edition.

Your team

A team of four (4) people minimum will represent a university or school. However, it's strongly recommended that two pairs should be formed by team: Aerodynamic/propulsion and structure.

The members of the team must be registred in a Master degree. A team may have additional L3 members or scientific club members.
